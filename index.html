About Us

Who we are ...

Officer and District Residents Association Incorporated (No. A0111161I) (ODRA) is an independent association created and managed by a group of volunteer Residents from Officer and the surrounding districts. There are already many thriving Residents and Community groups in this area which provide valuable support to their local communities. There are, however, other areas and communities which do not have the benefit of such groups. ODRA has been formed to provide this support by offering membership to anyone within Officer and surrounding districts no matter whether they rent, share or own their homes. We also see co-ordination and co-operation with existing, and future, residents and community groups as adding to the depth and quality of support provided across the whole district.

Our Vision

Make Officer a community where everyone is welcomed and valued.

Our Mission

Create an infrastructure that encompasses and deploys the skills, passions and values of the Residents of Officer.
